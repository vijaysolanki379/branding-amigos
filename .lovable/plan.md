

# Branding Amigos — Digital Marketing Agency Homepage

## Design Vision
A dark, futuristic single-page website with neon accent colors (cyan/electric blue), glassmorphism card effects, bold typography, and smooth micro-animations. Mobile-first, fast-loading, and production-ready.

---

## Sections to Build

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Sticky transparent header with glass effect on scroll
- Logo + nav links (Services, Process, Results, Testimonials)
- "Get Free Strategy Call" CTA button
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with professional team image background
- Animated gradient overlay with subtle movement
- **H1:** "AI-Powered Marketing That Builds Brands & Drives Revenue"
- Subheading text
- Two CTA buttons: "Get Free Strategy Call" and "View Services"
- Fade-in entrance animations

### 3. Why Choose Branding Amigos
- Strategy meeting image alongside content
- 4 glassmorphism cards with neon icon accents:
  - AI-Driven Strategies
  - ROI Focused Campaigns
  - Transparent Reporting
  - Affordable Growth Plans

### 4. Services Section
- Professional image of marketers at work
- 6 glassmorphism service cards with hover glow effects:
  - AI SEO Optimization
  - Social Media Marketing
  - Meta Ads Management
  - Google Ads Management
  - WhatsApp Marketing Automation
  - Local SEO

### 5. Process Timeline
- Team planning image
- Horizontal/vertical timeline with 5 steps: Discover → Plan → Launch → Optimize → Scale
- Animated step connectors with neon glow

### 6. Results / Stats Section
- Happy business owners image
- 4 animated counters that count up on scroll:
  - 250% Avg Traffic Growth
  - 4X ROI
  - 100+ Campaigns
  - 90% Retention Rate

### 7. Testimonials
- Client portrait images with glassmorphism quote cards
- Carousel/slider layout with smooth transitions
- Star ratings and client names/roles

### 8. Final CTA Section
- Agency meeting image background
- "Ready To Grow Your Business?" headline
- "Book Free Consultation" button
- Opens contact form modal

### 9. Footer
- Company info, quick links, social icons
- Clean minimal dark design

### 10. Contact Form Modal
- Triggered by CTA buttons throughout the page
- Fields: Name, Email, Phone, Message
- Form validation with user feedback

---

## Design Details
- **Color palette:** Dark background (#0a0a0f), neon cyan (#00f0ff) and electric purple accents, white text
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif headings, clean body text
- **Cards:** Semi-transparent backgrounds with backdrop blur (glassmorphism)
- **Images:** High-quality Unsplash professional business/marketing photos, lazy-loaded
- **Animations:** Fade-in on scroll, hover glow effects, gradient animation, counter animations
- **SEO:** Proper H1→H3 hierarchy, semantic HTML, meta-ready structure
